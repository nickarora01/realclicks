How invisible braces work

The Invisible Braces treatment consists of series of clear, removable aligners that shift your teeth into a straighter position. Week-by-week, the teeth are adjusted toward a straighter, more confident smile. The patient will wear an Invisible Braces aligner for around two weeks before progressing to the next set. Each aligner is designed to gradually move teeth into place, with patients collecting new aligner sets every six to eight weeks at their Invisible Braces check-up at Dental Avenue.
